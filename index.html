About

Leading care, education, and innovation

Dr. Fahad Alam is a staff anesthesiologist at Sunnybrook Health Sciences Centre, Associate Professor at the University of Toronto, and Medical and Research Director of the Sunnybrook Canadian Simulation Centre.

His work spans anesthesia, hyperbaric medicine, cosmetic complication management, simulation, medical education, human factors, and healthcare innovation. He has built programs, led committees, secured research funding, and contributed to teaching and scholarship across Canada and internationally.

In addition to his clinical and academic roles, Dr. Alam is a frequent invited speaker, moderator, workshop leader, and mentor whose work bridges patient care, leadership, and education.

Innovation

Innovation that improves healthcare, education, and patient outcomes

Dr. Alam’s innovation work focuses on improving how healthcare is delivered, taught, and experienced. His interests span simulation, human factors, operating room ergonomics, patient safety, hyperbaric medicine, cosmetic complications, and medical education.

He works at the intersection of clinical care, education, and innovation, helping teams adopt new approaches that improve learning, performance, and patient outcomes. While immersive technologies remain part of his work, they represent only one component of a broader commitment to advancing healthcare systems and education.
